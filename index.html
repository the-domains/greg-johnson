Using Big Data to Predict Brexit

Using Python and R, I accessed and analyzed data from popular immigration-related petitions on the UK’s online platform. I then used QGIS to map one million signers by constituency. Results indicate strong correlation to Brexit referendum election results. My visualizations were presented by Dr. Margetts for her May 2016 Political Turbulence talks at Harvard and MIT.
