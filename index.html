Using Big Data to Map Immigration Sentiment

Using Python and R, I accessed and analyzed JSON data from petitions on Westminster’s online platform. I then used QGIS to map one million signers by constituency. My visualizations were presented by Dr. Margetts for her May 2016 Political Turbulence talks at Harvard and MIT.
